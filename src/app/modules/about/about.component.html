About Us

Building with integrity. Growing with purpose.

More than 30 years in the real estate market

Established in 2005 by Anil Dua, Balaji Real Estate has served as a trusted cornerstone of the Mumbai property market for over 25 years. We began with a singular mission: to simplify the complexities of industrial and commercial real estate through deep market expertise and integrity. Today, that vision has evolved into a comprehensive consultancy that turns property goals into reality.

We have grown into recognized leaders providing end-to-end solutions, ranging from small-scale industrial galas to premium luxury residential housing. Our strategic expertise now spans the vital Mumbai-Gujarat corridor, including Vasai, Vapi, and Valsad, while extending into the prestigious international markets of Dubai to serve a diverse and global clientele.

Beyond mere transactions, we solve complex infrastructure challenges to help businesses and families thrive. From restoring value in established neighborhoods to marketing state-of-the-art high-rises, our commitment to transparency and a "stress-free" experience remains our guiding principle. At Balaji Real Estate, we don't just find spaces; we build lasting trust.

Doing right by those who trust isn’t a strategy — it’s the only way we know how to work.
